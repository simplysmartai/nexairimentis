How we treat your data

Data we collect. Email submissions, contact forms, and workflow requests are stored in Airtable + n8n logs so we can reply. We don’t sell lists and we purge inactive records every 12 months.

Cookies & analytics. We currently run privacy-friendly analytics only (no retargeting pixels). If that changes, we’ll update this page and surface a consent banner.

AI usage. Our agents can see the prompts/forms you submit. Humans still review anything before publishing or responding. Do not send sensitive info.

Affiliate disclosure. Some links earn commissions via Awin, Skimlinks, or direct partnerships. These programs use cookies to track purchases attributed to Nexairi.

Contact. Email hello@nexairi.com for data requests or to audit our workflows.
